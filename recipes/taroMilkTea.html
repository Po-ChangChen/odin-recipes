Taro Milk Tea

Taro milk tea is a delicious drink made from tapioca pearls and milk and taro and tea.

Ingredients

  • Boba (amount is how much you want to eat)
  • Milk
  • Tea leaves or tea bag
  • Hot water
  • Ice or refrigerator and patience
  • Taro flavor

Steps

  1. Make tea using hot water.
  2. Cool down the tea using ice or refrigerator.
  3. Add milk to the tea to create milk tea.
  4. Add taro flavor to milk tea to get taro milk tea.
  5. Add your boba.
  6. Enjoy!
